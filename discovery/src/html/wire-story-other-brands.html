USA recently launched a redesigned Mr. Robot page with The Verge article section. All the articles from The Verge are ingested into the CMS as a Post content type, with the author field auto-filled with "The Verge". Content editor can freely edit the content of the article.
However, the body is ingested with all the html code, which makes it difficult to edit the content. The content director mostly changes the title to the title case and bold/italicizes when applicable.
