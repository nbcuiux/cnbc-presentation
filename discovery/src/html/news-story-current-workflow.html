Creating a News Story Current Workflow
News Story
Current Workflow
From the initial screen, writer selects Create From Contemplate option at the top bar. She selects News Story content type.
Contemplate is a compound of content and template. It allows the writers to reuse the format and assets of a specific content. Contemplate is useful for quickly creating similar articles in the same category.
All the contemplates for the News Story appears in the search result in the left panel. She can further narrow the result down by using the search tool at the bottom.
After finding the contemplate to use, she clicks on the T button to create from contemplate.
This is the initial screen of the new News Story.
The required Slug field can be filled out either from the top of the entire news story or from the Core tab. Inside the tab, the UI shows the character count and if the slug is unique.
Back in the Content tab, writer enters the following basic information:
  • Title: Title of the news story.
  • SEO Title: The title to be searched by the search engines. It also becomes the URL of the news story.
  • Deck: This is a phrase or a sentence summarizing the news story.
In the Body section, writer can write the story in the boxes.
Each box represents a paragraph, and they separate out the paragraph in the same manner even when the text is pasted in.
On the right side of the paragraphs, there are several components the writer can insert for the section. All of the following components appear above the body text:
  • Subtitle: Appears as a heading of the section.
  • Embedded Assets: Images or videos.
  • Pull Quote: Pre-formatted pull-quote. On the front-end page, The title appears after the name and a comma.
To add additional embedded assets, writer has to search for the appropriate asset in the CMS. At the bottom bar, she selects Image as the filter.
To narrow down the result further, she also enters the keyword, Hillary Clinton, in the leftmost field and hits the Return key. The left pannel displays the result of the search.
She drags and drops the asset she would like to use from the left panel to the Embedded Assets box. She can add multiple assets in the box, which will appear in order on the front-end page.
Writer can create more body groups by clicking on the plus icon on the top-right corner to add additional components in between the paragraphs.
In this example, she added bullet points to illustrate 12 facts. Since Toolbelt has semantic displays, this view merely shows that these are the bullet point items and would be rendered based on the front-end rule on the website.
If she wants to delete a group of paragraphs, she can select the section via the checkbox on the top-right corner of the group, and select the minus icon on the sticky toolbar.
There is a confirmation dialog to prevent from deleting the group accidentally.
Towards the bottom of the Content tab, there is a Franchise section where writers and editors can assign one or more categories/sections for the article to appear.
Primary Assets have all the assets that are embedded in the body.
After done writing and editing the story, writer saves the news story by clicking the Save button on the bottom-left of the screen. It gives a confirmation message on the top and displays the Publish and Preview buttons.
There are Desktop, Mobile, and Content preview options under the Preview button.
This is the Desktop preview of the article.
This is the Mobile preview of the article. It appears in the new tab as a full-sized screen, but the mobile experience can be simulated with the smaller window size.
If the preview is satisfactory, editor clicks on Publish button to publish the news story. The dialog box on the left appears for confirmation.
