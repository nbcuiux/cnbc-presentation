This is an example of a wire story. In the core tab, you can see that the Created by section is marked with ingest, indicating that the article is ingested from the external source.
This is the Content tab view. Title and SEO Title fields are auto-completed, and the URL automatically appends the source in front of the title - reuters-america, in this example).
After editing the body of the article, the editors credit themselves at the very bottom paragraph.
Wire stories are assigned to the Wires Franchise by default, but the editor can assign more Franchises.
In the Attribution tab, the source is identified as Reuters. The Creator overwrite is set with the original writers of the article. Since the profile of the writers from other sources wouldn't exist as a node in the CMS, the overwrite field is a free text field.
